Description

Run and shoot your way out of Neon District in an infinite runner that has you jumping, sliding, doing parkour moves, shooting and reloading. You'll need both focus and dexterity to overcome the challenges in front of you.

Features

  • Fast & fluid gameplay in a neon-style scifi procedurally generated track
  • Get in the zone by timing your jumps, slides, laser shots and reloads
  • Pull some badass front flips, swan dives or wallruns
  • Get psyched with the synthwave soundtrack and environment beating in sync
  • Grab armor and powerful weapons to shred your enemies into pieces
  • Collect coins to unlock new equipment
  • Every run is different!
  • Compete for high score with your Play Games friends... and the world!
